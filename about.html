Who We Are

Migori County Referral Hospital is a vital healthcare institution serving the people of Migori County and neighboring regions. With decades of medical excellence, we provide comprehensive, accessible, and patient-centered care using modern technology and compassionate service.

We are committed to the highest standards of ethics and professionalism while responding to the evolving needs of our growing population.
