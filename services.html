Our Departments & Services

Comprehensive care with modern technology and compassionate service.

Departments

Casualty

Emergency trauma care operating 24/7 with rapid response, stabilization, and triage protocols integrated with county emergency services.

Outpatient

Handles general consultations, chronic condition reviews, minor procedures, and referrals to specialist clinics.

Eye Centre

Dedicated to diagnosing and treating eye conditions including cataracts, glaucoma, and refractive errors. Offers minor surgeries and screening camps.

Laboratory

Modern diagnostics including hematology, microbiology, blood transfusion, biochemistry, and rapid disease testing.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation for orthopedic injuries, stroke recovery, post-operative care, and chronic pain management.

Maternity & Newborn

Comprehensive maternal and newborn care including delivery suites, NICU, antenatal, postnatal, and family planning services.

Radiology

Imaging services including X-rays, ultrasound, and fluoroscopy. Supports diagnosis and surgical planning.

CT Centre

Advanced computed tomography scans to aid diagnosis of complex conditions including trauma, cancer, and vascular issues.

ENT

Treats ear, nose, and throat conditions. Services include minor surgeries, audiology, and allergy testing.

CCC (Comprehensive Care Clinic)

Specialized HIV/AIDS care, TB screening, adherence counseling, and psychosocial support. Linked with national HIV programs.

ICT Department

Maintains the hospital’s digital systems including Taifa Care biometric tablets, NHIF portals, and KHIS interoperability.

Central Records

Custodian of patient records, manages digitization, e-claims processing, NHIF linkage, and physical file archives.

Mother and Child Health

Provides immunization, antenatal clinics, growth monitoring, and family planning services under one integrated unit.

Our Services

Community Health & Outreach

We engage the community through screenings, health talks, deworming, maternal care visits, and coordination with CHVs.

SHA Digital Claims – Taifa Care

MCRH has adopted SHA’s Taifa Care system using biometric tablets launched in 2024 to support real-time cashless care claims.

Nutrition & Dietary Services

Specialized diet planning for diabetes, hypertension, maternal cases, and malnutrition recovery.

Specialist Clinics

Outpatient care by in-house and visiting consultants including surgeons, ENT specialists, cardiologists, and dermatologists.
